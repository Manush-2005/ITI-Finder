About College

INDUSTRIAL TRAINING INSTITUTE-ITI, SARKHEJ (Government) was established In 1979. It is approved by DGET (Directorate General of Training), NCVT (National Council on Vocational Training) & affiliated by GR24000108. The campus of the INDUSTRIAL TRAINING INSTITUTE-ITI, SARKHEJ (Government) is located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. The Institute can be reached either by rail or by road. The mission of the INDUSTRIAL TRAINING INSTITUTE-ITI, SARKHEJ (Government) is to contribute to nation and society at the highest international levels of excellence and to stand on the same scaffold with other universities of eminence.
