What is the ITI?

In India, ITI, which stands for Industrial Training Institute, is a crucial component of the country's vocational education system. ITIs offer a wide range of skill development programs and courses in fields such as engineering, mechanics, electronics, and more. These institutions focus on practical training, equipping students with hands-on skills essential for employment in diverse industries. ITIs play a pivotal role in addressing the nation's skilled labor shortage and fostering economic growth by providing the workforce with valuable technical expertise and employability opportunities.
